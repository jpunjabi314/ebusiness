Local Attractions

The Ocean's Pearl is conveniently located near a variety of local attractions, making it the perfect choice for both leisure and business travelers. Here are some of the top attractions in the area:


Beach

Ocean City, NJ is a pristine beach town that boasts some of the most beautiful and well-maintained beaches on the East Coast. From its sprawling stretches of powdery white sand to its crystal-clear waters, Ocean City's beaches offer visitors an idyllic seaside experience that is second to none.
The town's beachfront is known for its family-friendly atmosphere, with a range of activities to keep visitors of all ages entertained. Whether you want to bask in the sun, swim in the ocean, or try your hand at surfing, Ocean City's beaches have something for everyone.


Boardwalk

Ocean City offers a charming boardwalk that stretches for miles along the coastline. The boardwalk is home to a wide range of shops, restaurants, and attractions, including amusement parks, mini-golf courses, and arcades.


Castaway Cove

Castaway Cove is an amusement park located in Ocean City, New Jersey, USA. It offers a variety of rides and attractions suitable for all ages, from thrilling roller coasters to gentle kiddie rides.
One of the park's most popular rides is the GaleForce roller coaster, which reaches speeds of up to 64 miles per hour and features a beyond-vertical drop.


Gillian's Wonderland

Gillian's Wonderland Pier is a family-friendly amusement park located on the boardwalk in Ocean City, New Jersey, USA. The park is named after its founder, David Gillian, who opened the park in 1929.
Gillian's Wonderland offers a variety of rides and attractions suitable for all ages, including classic amusement park rides like the Ferris Wheel, the Tilt-A-Whirl, and the Scrambler. There are also several kid-friendly rides, such as the Convoy, the Dizzy Dragons, and the Happy Feet.


Mini Golf

Ocean City Mini Golf is a family-friendly miniature golf course located in Ocean City, New Jersey, USA. The course is situated on the boardwalk, offering beautiful ocean views as players make their way through the 18 holes.
The course is designed with a variety of themes, featuring obstacles such as waterfalls, bridges, and caves. Each hole presents a unique challenge, testing players' putting skills and creativity.
